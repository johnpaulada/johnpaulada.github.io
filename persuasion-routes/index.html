[Video] Persuasion Routes

13 June 2016 on social, experiment, video

There are two "routes" used in persuasion: the central route and the peripheral route. Briefly speaking, central route involves informing the target entity of the benefits/features of doing or having something, convincing them that it is actually beneficial to them, while the peripheral persuasion route relies on other techniques aside from demonstrating the features and benefits to grab the the attention and attract the target.

After learning this, we were asked to create two minute-long advertisement videos -- one that uses the central route and one that uses the peripheral route.
