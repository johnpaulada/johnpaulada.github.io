Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei Light Novels

19 July 2016 on pdf, anime, mahouka, mahouka koukou no rettousei, light novel, epub

Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei is set in a world where the once arcane magic is now a science. An underestimated "Weed" big brother and an overachieving "Bloom" sister goes to the First High School as first year students. Events upon events gradually reveal who the siblings are.

The first season of Mahouka only covers the first few books and there is so much more to the story. Interested? Here is a collection of the light novels. in ePub and PDF formats.
