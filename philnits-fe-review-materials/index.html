PhilNITS (FE) Review Materials

13 June 2016 on resources, it, cs, pdf, slides, books

Just because IT and Computer-related courses do not have a board exam doesn't mean that there are no certifications. Aside from TESDA, there's also the PhilNITS Foundation which, according to their website, is a:

non-stock, non-profit, non-government organization that is implementing an I.T Certification Program from I.T. standards adopted from Japan, with the support of the Department of Trade & industry (DTI) of the Philippines and the Ministry of Economy, Trade & Industry (METI) of Japan.

You may ask, "If it's adopted from Japan standards, is it difficult then?". YES IT IS. According to their website, the Fundamental IT Engineer (FE) Exam has a passing rate of 14.2%.

What. The. Actual. Fuck.

The IT Passport (IP) Exam has a passing rate of 21.7% while the Applied IT Engineer (AP) Exam has a passing rate of 23.11%. Better, but still low.

But fear not!
